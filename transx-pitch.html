Built for Kigali.
Designed for Every Youth.

Trans-X addresses a critical mobility gap faced by students, youth, and underserved communities in Kigali — where 500+ buses run daily without a single intelligent coordination layer. We change that.

📉
Reduces waiting time by predicting bus arrivals with AI — helping students arrive on time to school and work.
🗺
Covers all 7 corridors and 40+ routes across Kigali — including new routes to SEZ, Bumbogo, and Shyorongi.
💳
Integrates with Ecofleet Tap & Go — adding MoMo top-up and QR ticketing without replacing existing hardware.
📊
Gives operators and RURA real-time data dashboards for smarter fleet management and urban planning.
